<h2>UCI's favorite books to read all in one place.</h2>
<hr />

<p>The renowned faculty at the University of California, Irvine have spent many years growing and cultivating their knowledge to become some of the leading authorities in their respective fields. They have gained this expertise through hard work, research, persistence, and of course plenty of reading.</p>
<p>UCI professors submit their own recommendations on what to read in order to stay ahead. They share the books that helped them reach where they are today. Whether you are a student who wants to expand his horizons, a professor who needs to share an astounding new publication with the masses, or are just curious about how our faculty has become the experts they are today; Booklist is for you.</p>
<p>Booklist is here to give students and faculty a head start on building their knowledge. Follow the exact literary path your teachers and colleagues took before you in order to gain the information necessary to forge ahead with your own journey. Booklist is an example of what can be achieved when students and faculty work together to create a better educational experience.</p>
